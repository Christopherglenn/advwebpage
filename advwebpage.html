Me

I was born in Stamford Connecticut. Though Stamford was my birth place I spent most of my life in Bridgeport Connecticut. I went to multiple schools in Bridgeport some of which were Read School, Wilbur L. Cross, and Central Magnet Highschool where I really began my journey into art. I took 4 years of photoshop throughout highschool and just loved everything about it. I was always a good drawer but I never thought about working on a computer. This really designated for me and I eventually started looking at more things computers had to offer. Then taking a course in computer writing change everything for me. That class was very hard but I never strayed from trying to accomplish the goals I set for myself. I had not chosen what I wanted to be but I knew I wanted to be on a computer. UCONN Stamford introduced to my the Digital Media and Design program in which I fell in love with. I have chosen Web Design as my major but it doesn't mean that is all I intend to perfect.
